The file-able entity representing the etoy.  Also is subclassable by the author of a particular etoy, with the intent being to centralize all the needs authors may have to subclass our generic e-toy-related classes in one place; consult, for example, the "hooks" and "scaffolding" categories, and look at the DriveACar subclass for an examples of a particular etoy customizing behavior. 

After making a new subclass of EToyHolder, start building in it by executing:
	EToyPlayer openOn: YourClass new.

For writing on the disk, notInPlayfield contains the objects held in the world that are not in the playfield, that do not have the property 'eToyControls'.  standardPlayer is one.
